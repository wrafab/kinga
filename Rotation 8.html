Soldering

To solder means to join metal surfaces, in our case, using lead and tin. We use a soldering iron to melt the solder and make electrical connections.

Soldering

I really enjoyed soldering. It was something I never thought I would be doing. It was awesome to solder different parts onto the circuit board, plug the battery in, and have the LED light up.
