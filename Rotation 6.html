Vinyl Cutting

For vinyl cutting we were allowed to print whatever we wanted. Vinyl can be used to make stickers, posters, signs, and more. I decided to make two stickers. One of Lion's wide receiver, Golden Tate, and another of my new puppy. I used CorelDraw to create the pictures and then I printed them on The WRA Roland GS-24 printer.
